About Us

Mileage is a project that was born as the thesis of a master's degree in data science of five colleagues. The project starts from a database of second-hand vehicles and is fully developed to offer a service to the customer. It goes through the phases of: treatment and processing of the original data, screening and enrichment of variables, feature engineering, creation of different models, selection of the best model, deployment of the model to an application and implementation of the application on a web page.
